Instafun

Instafun is a node-express-bootstrap application that uses the Instagram API to access real-time photos
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

It is a great way to watch the world unfold real time via Instagram. Users generally upload photos right away to Instagram after they take them, since there is no way to save a work-in-progress. When you pull the photos by tags, you could travel from wondrous city to city, lust after gastronomic dish to dish, and explore the world in real time. Enjoy!
